The best pet.

Bunnies are small, furry mammals with long ears, short fluffy tails, and strong, large hind legs.

Why you should get a bunny.

  1. They are too cute!
  2. They are very energetic.
  3. They love to play.
  4. They are very independent

Why you shouldn't get a bunny.

  1. They live about 8-12 years.
  2. They are very sensitive.
  3. They are not the best pets for children
  4. They could possibly scratch you.
